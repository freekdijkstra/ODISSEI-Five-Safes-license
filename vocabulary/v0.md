# TRE Vocabulary

Namespace: `https://w3id.org/tre/vocabulary/v0#` (prefix `tre:`)

An ODRL Profile that defines the terms needed to write access policies for Trusted Research Environments (TREs), such as the ODISSEI Five Safes License.

## Classes

| Term                           | Label                          | Definition                                                                                                                            |
|--------------------------------|--------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `tre:TRE`                      | Trusted Research Environment   | A controlled compute environment in which permitted analyses on a dataset may be performed, without the data leaving the environment. |

## Actions

| Term                           | Label                          | Definition                                                                                                     |
|--------------------------------|--------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `tre:analyse`                  | Run an analysis on a dataset   | The permission of running an analysis on a dataset. Specialisation of odrl:grantUse.                           |
| `tre:declareIntendedPurpose`   | Declare intended purpose       | The duty to declare the intended purpose of access. Specialisation of odrl:inform.                             |
| `tre:provideAccessPurpose`     | Provide access purpose         | The duty to provide a description of the purpose for which access is requested. Specialisation of odrl:inform. |
| `tre:acceptTerms`              | Accept terms of use            | The duty to accept the terms of use. Specialisation of odrl:obtainConsent.                                     |
